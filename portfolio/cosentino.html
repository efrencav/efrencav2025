Cosentino – Silestone & Dekton Campaigns

Brand Storytelling, Print Collateral, Packaging, Digital Design

As Art Director, Efren Cavazos led the creative development of multiple marketing initiatives for Cosentino, the global leader in architectural surfaces. The work focused on elevating the Silestone and Dekton brands across North American and Latin American markets through premium print collateral, packaging solutions, and targeted digital campaigns.

Efren managed a cross-functional in-house team of designers, copywriters, and a copy editor, while also directing external vendors including photographers and stylists. From product launch kits and showroom collateral to packaging labels and campaign materials, each touchpoint was designed to align with Cosentino’s refined brand identity while reinforcing product differentiation in a highly competitive market.

He also art directed several product photography sessions—coordinating with local teams across sites to ensure brand consistency and visual excellence. The result was a cohesive body of work that blended strategic positioning with upscale aesthetics, helping Silestone and Dekton stand out in retail environments, trade show settings, and design industry publications.
