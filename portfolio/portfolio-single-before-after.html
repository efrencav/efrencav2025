Elevating Wellness: A Refined Rebrand for Enfusia

Efren Cavazos led the complete rebrand of Enfusia, a bath and body company seeking a refreshed, modern identity to reflect its growth and evolving market position. The goal was to create a look and feel that captured the brand’s artisanal quality while elevating its presence in both retail and online spaces.

The rebrand included a new visual identity, featuring an updated logo, refined typography, and a sophisticated color palette designed to communicate wellness, quality, and trust. Efren documented these elements in a comprehensive brand guidelines manual, detailing logo usage, color specifications, typography hierarchy, and design applications to ensure consistency across all marketing and packaging materials.

The result was a cohesive, upscale brand system that provided Enfusia with a clear creative direction—positioning the company for stronger recognition and long-term growth.
