Café Diario – Branding & Packaging Design

Brewing a Bold Identity: Branding & Packaging for Café Diario

Efren Cavazos led the complete brand development for Café Diario, a specialty coffee brand seeking to establish a distinctive presence in a crowded market. Starting from the ground up, he directed the creation of a cohesive visual identity that captured the brand’s artisanal quality and approachable sophistication. This included logo design, typography selection, color palette, and supporting graphic elements designed for consistent application across all platforms.

The core of the project focused on packaging design for multiple coffee varieties. Efren developed layouts that balanced shelf impact with storytelling—integrating origin details, roast profiles, and brand narrative in a way that was both visually engaging and easy for customers to navigate. He ensured every packaging element adhered to production requirements while maintaining the integrity of the brand’s aesthetic.

In addition to packaging, Efren oversaw the design of supporting marketing collateral such as point-of-sale materials, product sheets, and promotional displays. By blending strategic brand positioning with refined design execution, he helped Café Diario build a recognizable identity that resonates with both retail buyers and end consumers.
